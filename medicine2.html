Why Medicine Donation?

According to WHO, around 469 million people in India do not have regular access to essential medicines and medical supplies. Barely 20% of the total population can afford the required medical treatments and medicines. In such times, it is necessary to find a way out or at least try to make sure that people in need of medicines and suppliers get access to it. One way is to donate unused medicines.
